Miracle Messages had a daunting goal – they wanted to inspire volunteers around the world to join their global effort to end homelessness. Based in San Francisco, Miracle Messages knew that they were projecting a site specific identity and needed a way to not only show their global footprint, but also allow for further expansion. To reach that goal our Lamba Lab team created a simple, yet elegant solution. We constructed a map app to display the locations of existing chapters with contact information embedded, as well as resources cues in unserved areas to create new chapters.

From the depths of despair comes a gut wrenching need - TO RANT! Life is tough. Relationships are tough. School - tough. Work - tough. Being able to let off steam and release those grievances is healthy and necessary to thriving in this sometimes frantic and difficult life. What better way to let loose on the universe than to let it all out down a virtual Blackhole?! Cranky client? Demanding spouse? Never ending deadlines? Shout them all out and throw them headfirst down the Backhole into oblivion. The release is real and the effects on others are zilch. You didn’t yell your head off and you didn’t make a fool out of yourself. The Blackhole will never divulge your innermost anxieties and rage... unless you want a gentle reminder later to reassure yourself that it all worked out. The Blackhole is just what you need to survive this crazy world. Blackhole is a Lamba team app designed to vent rage and frustration. Simple, effective and confidential.

Ah, the good old days of exchanging business cards and tucking them neatly away in your pocket or purse to later file in a spinning wheel of information - a Roladex. But realistically who can carry a thirty pound Roladex of faded and crumpled business cards everywhere they go? And how to organize? By first name, By company? By business type? And how do you easily share that information via your network of coworkers, clients and suppliers? The Last Roladex is the answer. Chunk that archaic piece of 19th century lore away and store all your business contacts in one easily accessible always near you place - your phone. The Last Roladex utilizes QR codes and shareable networks, making storing and sharing business contacts as easy as the touch of the screen. Store thousands of contacts and organize in multiple formats and share in an instant. It will truly be your Last Roladex. A Lamba team app.
