Miriacle Messages

To end homelessness, we must rebuild social homes as much as affordable housing. Miracle Messages reunites the homeless with their loved ones, and the rest of us as their neighbors.

Miracle Messages reached out to the lambda labs team with a simple problem, how do we show new volunteers that our platform is global? Our solution was simple, using the Mapbox api and the MM teams data we constructed a map to show the current locations of that the Miracle Messages team has a defined chapter and resources to help contact current or create a new chapter.
